Latest Ratings

Inception


Inception (2010), directed by Christopher Nolan, is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller that delves into the nature of dreams and reality. The film centers on Dom Cobb (played by Leonardo DiCaprio), a skilled "extractor" who specializes in stealing secrets from people's subconscious minds while they dream. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he successfully performs "inception"—the act of planting an idea in someone's mind without them realizing it.

Attack of titan

Attack on Titan (originally Shingeki no Kyojin) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. It was first serialized in Bessatsu Shōnen Magazine in 2009 and ran until 2021. The series has been adapted into multiple seasons of anime, which became hugely popular worldwide.

Dark

Dark is a German science fiction thriller series on Netflix, created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese. Premiering in December 2017, it became one of the most critically acclaimed series on Netflix, known for its complex narrative, intricate character arcs, and mind-bending exploration of time travel, family dynamics, and existential questions.
